About Us

The Food Wastage Management System is a web-based solution designed to tackle the growing problem of food waste in various sectors such as restaurants, supermarkets, households, and food banks. The system aims to minimize food waste by tracking surplus food, managing inventory, and facilitating donations or redistribution to people in need.

This project is aimed at helping users reduce food wastage, save costs, and contribute to a more sustainable and socially responsible food ecosystem. It also aligns with global sustainability goals, including reducing hunger and minimizing food-related carbon footprints.
